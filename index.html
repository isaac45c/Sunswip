About Sunswip

Sunswip is the artistic project of Isaac Cheurfi, a young independent artist and producer based near Paris, France.

The music is a blend of electro pop, EDM, and techno, focusing on emotional storytelling, melodic layers, and energetic production.

Inspired by artists like Avicii, Alan Walker, and David Guetta, Sunswip aims to create music that speaks to a generation raised on dancefloors and digital culture alike.

I compose and produce all my music myself, with a vision to connect deeply with listeners both live and online.
