About Sunswip

Sunswip is an independent artist and producer from near Paris, France, creating a unique blend of electro pop, EDM, and techno music. Inspired by artists like Avicii, Alan Walker, and David Guetta, Sunswip aims to create music that speaks to a generation raised on dancefloors and digital culture alike.

I compose and produce all my music myself, with a vision to connect deeply with listeners both live and online.

  • Started music production at age 14
  • Released multiple tracks and demos on BandLab
  • Built an engaged online community on social platforms
  • Currently seeking collaboration and label opportunities
