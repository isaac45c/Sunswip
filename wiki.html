Origins

Breach Place was founded in 2021, with the goal of blending nu-metal, rap, and electronic sounds. The band quickly grew a reputation for their explosive live shows and heartfelt lyrics about personal struggles and societal issues.

Formed in Paris, the band originally started as a group of friends who came together to create a new sound, with heavy influences from Linkin Park, Korn, and early 2000s alternative music. Each member brings their unique touch to the music, from intense guitar riffs to soulful rapping and atmospheric synths.

Small Anecdotes

  • Our first concert was a small local gig, where we played to a crowd of only 20 people. It was nerve-wracking, but it felt like the start of something big.
  • We once accidentally left our tour bus key in a hotel lobby, and had to scramble to get back in while the rest of the band made jokes about our "rock star" behavior.
  • One of our first songs, "ILYSM," was written in just a few hours during a jam session. It remains one of our fan favorites to this day!
